MY FAVORITE HOBBY

Yes, I love to read books because it is one of my hobby .It helps to feed our mind .Moreover books are the best companion.Books mean a lot to me. For me its an important part of reading to be interested and indulged in the book and to savour each and every line.We report on a study of children and parents shared reading of interactive printed books. We investigated the differences between books with interactive features and books with expressive typography in order to evaluate which features within a book encouraged interaction between the reading participants and the book. 11 parent and child groups took part in the study that involved three observed reading sessions.

MY INSPIRATION

“Sunrise” to kick off the day. “Boost” when I need to push hard/hustle/get productive. “Chill” for everyday occasions of any type. “Calm” to work in low-pace, or to lower stress. “Focus” when I need to think deeply. Understand that you can be an inspiration to others, no matter what you feel you have or have not achieved. Even if you feel that you have not yet reached the endpoint, and even if you feel like you have reached a low point, you have the ability to inspire. ... You will be amazed at the way inspiring others makes you feel.
